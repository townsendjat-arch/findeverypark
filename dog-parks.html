🐕 Dog Parks

Find Aurora's off-leash dog parks and dog-friendly local businesses in one place.

🐕 Dog Parks

Fenced off-leash dog parks in Aurora. Always carry waste bags and check posted rules at each location.

Grandview Dog Park
17500 E Quincy Ave
Fenced Off-Leash Water Separate Areas
Agility equipment; gets busy on weekends; gravel/dirt terrain.
Cherry Creek Dog Off-Leash Area
S Parker Rd & E Orchard Rd
Fenced Off-Leash Creek Swimming 107+ acres
Requires $3 daily or $25 annual DOLA permit + State Park Pass.
Bicentennial Dog Park
13600 E Bayaud Ave
Fenced Off-Leash Community Park
Dirt/crushed stone; good for a quick social visit. No water on-site.
Singletree Dog Park
17400 E Telluride St • 6.5 ac
Fenced Off-Leash Residential Area
Bring your own water and waste bags.
🏢 Dog-Friendly Businesses
Resolute Brewing Company
7281 S Yosemite St, Centennial, CO
Indoor & Patio
One of the most dog-friendly spots in the DTC area. Dogs allowed inside the taproom and on the turf patio with fire pit.
Living the Dream Brewing
9150 Commerce Center Cir #300, Highlands Ranch, CO
Indoor & Patio
Famous for being dog-centric. Dogs welcome inside the taproom and they often have a resident brew dog.
2 Penguins Tap and Grill
13065 E Briarwood Ave, Centennial, CO
Patio Only
Large 3,000 sq ft patio that welcomes dogs outside but not inside the dining room.
Lone Tree Brewing Company (Parker Location)
18425 Pony Express Dr, Parker, CO
Indoor & Patio
Dog-friendly taproom and patio. Leashed dogs welcome indoors.
Fiction Beer Company (Parker)
19523 Hess Rd #103, Parker, CO
Indoor & Patio
Very dog-friendly indoor space. They even offer a non-alcoholic dog beer on their dog menu.
Welcome Home Brewery
19523 Hess Rd Unit 103, Parker, CO
Indoor & Patio
Cozy living-room style brewery. Dogs welcome inside and outside.
Bodega Beer Company
19523 Hess Rd Unit 103, Parker, CO
Patio Only
Coffee in the morning and beer later. Dogs welcome on the shaded patio.
