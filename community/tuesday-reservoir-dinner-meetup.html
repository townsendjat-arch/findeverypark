Tuesday Reservoir Dinner Meetup

A casual outdoor dinner meetup for families, friends, neighbors, and anyone who wants to join us.

We've been doing this for about two years with friends, and this summer we're opening it up to the public. Bring dinner for your family, bring chairs or a blanket, and come hang out.

Sometimes it stays simple. Sometimes it turns into a big potluck. Either way, the goal is easy: good people, kids playing, and dinner outside.

Details

  • Dates: Tuesdays from May 26 through August 4, 2026
  • Time: 5:30 PM until people head out
  • Format: Rotating every other Tuesday between Cherry Creek Reservoir and Aurora Reservoir

Locations

Cherry Creek Reservoir — Swim Beach area

Aurora Reservoir

What to Bring

  • Food for your family
  • Chairs or a picnic blanket
  • Drinks
  • Sunscreen / bug spray
  • Kids, friends, and neighbors

Good to Know

This is casual and family-friendly. There is no formal program, no registration, and no pressure. Just show up, eat dinner, and enjoy being outside with other people.

Weather, park rules, parking, and entrance fees may apply depending on the reservoir.
