Parker dog parks — what to know

Parker's main off-leash dog park is large, well-maintained, and has separate areas for big and small dogs. It sits next to an 18-hole disc golf course, making it a solid stop even if the dog isn't joining. Water stations and agility features are on site.

Dogs are welcome on-leash throughout most Parker parks and open spaces. The trail network is one of the better setups in the area for longer dog walks, especially along Cherry Creek and the Sulphur Gulch corridor.
