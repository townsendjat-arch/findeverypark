## Simple Outreach List Format

Use the tracker CSV as the working list. Keep each row simple:
- `target_id`: stable ID for follow-up
- `category`: coffee, treats, lunch, swim, kids classes, or indoor play
- `cluster`: the Aurora park cluster that makes the sponsor pitch locally relevant
- `business_name`: business to contact
- `contact_channel`: best available public channel, usually contact form, listed email, phone, or Instagram
- `fit_note`: one sentence tying the business to Aurora family park planning
- `status`: not_started, contacted, replied, follow_up, won, not_fit
- `next_step`: the next manual action
