Aurora Reservoir

Address: 5800 S. Powhaton Rd.

The Real Breakdown

Aurora's crown jewel. An 8-mile paved loop circles the reservoir — flat, wide, and stroller-ready. The swim beach draws families all summer. Non-motorized boating, fishing, archery range, and a playground round it out. No shade on the trail itself, so bring hats and sunscreen.

Key Features

  • 1,861 acres
  • Swim beach
  • Fishing
  • 8-mile paved trail
  • Leashed dogs welcome
  • Boating
  • Archery
  • Large parking lot

Shelter

Reservable shelter available.
